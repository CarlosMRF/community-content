[Advanced RAG Hackathon with Vectara](https://lablab.ai/event/advanced-rag-hackathon) has successfully concluded, showcasing a remarkable array of innovative applications and chatbots developed with leading AI technologies. Hosted in collaboration with Vectara, LlamaIndex, Together AI, and Unstructured.io, the hackathon attracted 1205 participants across 206 teams, who pushed the boundaries of what's possible in AI application development.

## Hackathon **Challenges and Opportunities**

This hackathons’s challenge was to create RAG chatbots or question-answering applications for diverse industries such as legal, manufacturing, healthcare, retail, and insurance. Participants were encouraged to utilize Vectara's new chat functionality, the open-source Hallucination Evaluation Model (HHEM), LlamaIndex's APIs, Together AI’s tools, and Tonic AI's data analytics to create impactful GenAI applications that exemplify the potential of RAG-as-a-service.

## **The Hackathon Winners 🏆**

The hackathon spotlighted innovative projects that showcased the potential of AI in solving real-world problems:

1. **[MindPal](https://lablab.ai/event/advanced-rag-hackathon/puppeteers/mindpal-your-creative-brain)**: Welcome to MindPal, your personal creative assistant and second brain 🤖! MindPal is not just another note-taking app; it is designed to help you organize your thoughts and ideas effortlessly.
2. **[Garden Rulez](https://lablab.ai/event/advanced-rag-hackathon/garden-rulez/engaging-approach-to-community-gardening-and-ai)**:Community gardening in Germany is a deeply rooted tradition, offering significant benefits such as enhancing biodiversity, promoting social interaction, and contributing to food security. 
3. **[Legallit](https://lablab.ai/event/advanced-rag-hackathon/blazzbyte/legallit)**: Legalit is an AI-powered legal assistant that helps individuals and businesses easily review and understand legal contracts. By leveraging advanced natural language processing and machine learning, Legalit provides personalized insights and recommendations to ensure your contracts are compliant and protected.

### **Special Prizes Winners**

Noteworthy projects that won special prizes:

- **[Wisdom Hoard](https://lablab.ai/event/advanced-rag-hackathon/wisdom-hoard/wisdom-hoard)**: A universal bookmarking tool that doubles as a knowledge vault, utilizing Vectara for content management and summarization.
- **[IssueCopilot.AI](https://lablab.ai/event/advanced-rag-hackathon/elasticsearch/issuecopilot-ai)**: Automates ticket generation from customer reviews, directing them to appropriate developer teams, enhanced by LlamaIndex.

- **[Snowflake](https://lablab.ai/event/advanced-rag-hackathon/spacecats/snowflake)**: A personal assistant designed specifically for diabetes management.
- **[HireChat](https://lablab.ai/event/advanced-rag-hackathon/ai-powerhouse/hire-chat)**: Enhances recruitment processes by automating responses to candidate FAQs.

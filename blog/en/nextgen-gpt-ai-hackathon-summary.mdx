## **The Hackathon Winners üèÜ**

The hackathon spotlighted innovative projects that showcased the potential of AI in solving real-world problems:

1. **[Story Prep:](https://lablab.ai/event/nextgen-gpt-ai-hackathon/story-prep/story-prep)**: A web tool that generates vocabulary sheets and listening exercises from any fiction work, enhancing vocabulary acquisition.
2. **[AI Project Manager Assistant](https://lablab.ai/event/nextgen-gpt-ai-hackathon/jarvis/ai-project-manager-assistant)**: A Slack bot that records meeting transcripts and provides summaries, optimizing project management.
3. **[Recovery Pal](https://lablab.ai/event/nextgen-gpt-ai-hackathon/recoverypal/recovery-pal)**: An app that supports addiction recovery through AI-driven affirmations, journaling, art interpretation, and customized meditations.

### **Finalists**

The finalists presented projects that were both innovative and practical, addressing various needs and industries:

- **[Food Complain Resolution:](https://lablab.ai/event/nextgen-gpt-ai-hackathon/devstat-fusion/food-complaint-resolution)**: An AI-assisted application that streamlines the complaint resolution process in the food delivery market.
- **[Chatting with Historical Icons](https://lablab.ai/event/nextgen-gpt-ai-hackathon/chatting-with-historical-icons/chatting-with-historical-icons)**: A Generative AI application that aids learning by enabling conversations with historical figures.
- **[EyeCue](https://lablab.ai/event/nextgen-gpt-ai-hackathon/create/eyecue-inspiring-recruiting-videos-by-ai)**: An initiative to revolutionize recruitment through AI-generated inspirational videos, targeting passive job seekers on social media.

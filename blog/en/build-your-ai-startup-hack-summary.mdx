## **Event Overview**

[The Build your AI Startup Hackathon](https://lablab.ai/event/lablab-next-create-your-startup) was a spectacular convergence of creativity and innovation, drawing an impressive 6,609 participants organized into 356 dynamic teams. These dedicated individuals collaborated tirelessly to develop 84 cutting-edge AI applications. The event was a vibrant celebration of the burgeoning interest and immense potential in AI entrepreneurship, highlighting the remarkable talent and forward-thinking ideas driving the future of technology.

## **üåü Hackathon Challenge**

The Build your AI Startup Hackathon presented an exciting challenge, pushing participants to create fully functional prototypes using advanced AI models such as LLMs, AI Agents, and Text-to-Speech technologies. The goal was to harness the power of Generative AI to automate tasks, generate innovative content, and unlock new opportunities, driving the development of impactful solutions. This event was not only a test of technical skill but also a platform for entrepreneurial vision, offering participants the chance to turn their prototypes into market-ready products.

## **üèÜ Prizes and Opportunities**

Cash prizes were awarded to the top three teams, with $5,000 for 1st place, $3,000 for 2nd place, and $2,000 for 3rd place. Additionally, winners gained a fast-track to the interview process for the Lablab NEXT acceleration program. 

Lablab NEXT is a dynamic six-week accelerator program designed to catapult startup prototypes and MVPs (Minimum Viable Products) into the market. Participants in NEXT received intensive mentorship from seasoned professionals, ensuring they had the insights and guidance needed to succeed. The program offered robust support, including expert mentorship, market readiness strategies, extensive networking opportunities, and access to essential resources. This prestigious program provided a launchpad for transforming visionary concepts into successful startups, emphasizing the importance of innovation and market impact in the competitive business world.

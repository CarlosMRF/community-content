[The Cohere Coral Hackathon](https://lablab.ai/event/cohere-coral-hackathon) recently concluded, marking a significant achievement in the world of AI. With over two thousand expressing interest and **599 participants** forming **141 teams**, the event was a hub of creativity and innovation. A total of **38 AI applications** were developed, showcasing the vast potential of AI in various domains.

## Hackathon **Challenges and Opportunities**

Participants were presented with specific challenges, focusing on the best use of RAG in different modes. They were also given exclusive access to Co.chat + RAG (Beta) by Cohere, enhancing API capabilities with new modes.

The event offered attractive prizes, including tech gadgets, cash awards, and credits from Cohere and Weaviate, for the top winners.

## **The Hackathon Winners üèÜ**

The competition was fierce, but three projects stood out, claiming the top spots:

1. **[BYTE - AI-Based Nutrition App](https://lablab.ai/event/cohere-coral-hackathon/byte/byte-ai-based-nutrition-app)**: This groundbreaking app emerged as the winner, revolutionizing wellness and nutrition with its AI-driven approach. It integrates advanced technologies from Cohere, Cohere Embed, Weaviate, and LangChain.
2. **[CancerAI](https://lablab.ai/event/cohere-coral-hackathon/biologyai/cancerai)**: Securing the second place, CancerAI is a pioneering tool designed to empower bioinformatics researchers. It utilizes resources from Pubmed through Entrez, combined with AI generative models, featuring Cohere and ChatGPT integration.
3. **[OrbChat - Always-On Support for your Website](https://lablab.ai/event/cohere-coral-hackathon/coral-explorers/orbchat-always-on-support-for-your-website)**: Grabbing the third spot, OrbChat enhances website customer support with its AI-driven solution. It ensures uninterrupted service, allowing customers to choose between AI help and a human agent. The application is powered by technologies including Cohere, Cohere Generate, Cohere Embed, Weaviate, and LangChain.

### **Finalists**

The other Finalists Stream participants, though not in the top three, were acknowledged as finalists for their innovative contributions and the potential impact of their AI applications. Congrats to:

- **[RAG Fusion with Cohere and Weaviate](https://lablab.ai/event/cohere-coral-hackathon/thruthink/rag-fusion-with-cohere-and-weaviate)**: Enhancing query generation and document retrieval for more accurate database matches.
- **[Tadabor](https://lablab.ai/event/cohere-coral-hackathon/data-dreamers/tadabor)**: Aiding in the understanding of the Quran, making its teachings more accessible to everyone.

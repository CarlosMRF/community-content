# Cohere
Cohere is a leading AI company specializing in natural language processing (NLP) and large language models (LLMs) designed for enterprise applications. Founded by former Google researchers, Cohere focuses on creating AI tools that enhance business productivity by enabling better understanding, generation, and classification of text data. Cohere is known for its flexible and secure deployment options, allowing businesses to integrate AI into their systems seamlessly, whether through cloud-based, on-premises, or hybrid solutions.

## Useful Information and Key Dates

- **Founding**: Cohere was founded by Aidan Gomez, Ivan Zhang, and Nick Frosst, with the mission to make advanced AI accessible and scalable for businesses.
  
- **Key Milestones**:
  - **September 2021**: Cohere raised $40 million in Series A funding, marking its significant entry into the AI industry.
  - **June 2023**: The company raised $270 million in Series C funding, achieving a valuation of $2.2 billion, indicating its rapid growth and strong market position.
  - **March 2024**: Cohere reported an annualized revenue run rate of $22 million and aimed to expand further with additional funding.

## Main Technologies

Cohere's technological offerings are centered around generative AI and NLP, tailored for enterprise use. The primary models include:

1. **Command**: A model focused on generating and summarizing text, used for creating content like product descriptions, articles, and document summaries. For more information, check our [command tech page](https://lablab.ai/tech/cohere/command).
  
2. **Embed**: This model provides high-quality text embeddings for tasks such as semantic search and text classification, supporting over 100 languages. For more information, check our [embed tech page](https://lablab.ai/tech/cohere/embed).
  
3. **Rerank**: Part of the Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) framework, this model helps in ranking search results or responses to ensure the most relevant outcomes. For more information, check our [rerank tech page](https://lablab.ai/tech/cohere/rerank).
  
4. **Classify Models**: These are used to categorize text into predefined classes, useful for content moderation and sentiment analysis. For more information, check our [classify tech page](https://lablab.ai/tech/cohere/classify).

## Commitment to Responsible AI

Cohere has also been active in promoting responsible AI development. The company has signed onto voluntary codes of conduct in both the United States and Canada, ensuring its AI systems are developed and deployed ethically and transparently.
